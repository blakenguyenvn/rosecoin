FAQs

Questions and concerns answered

1. What is Whispers?

Whispers is an innovative one to one live streaming platform that allows any person from anywhere in the world to offer a video call with another user at any time for a pre-agreed on price. Both parties can search for dates based on their own criteria while remaining anonymous and secure. Whispers is decentralized dating, delivered.

2. What is live streaming?

Live streaming is a real time video call between 2 or more parties. Over the last year, live stream broadcasts have become very popular on social media and other platforms where hosts earn money from gifts for their streams, kind of like IG and FB stories but monetized without ads (e.g. bigo live, UpLive, 17). This is an evolution of the traditional content creator monetization strategy of advertisements and product placements on youtube and other platforms which require tens of thousands of viewers to actually start making any money. Whispers' direct calling is the next iteration of the one to many live streaming model as it is a more private, intimate and straighforward way to get rewarded by offering your time and attention to other users at a negotiable price.

3. Why use the Whispers platform?

Our platform is free to join and easy to use. Anyone, anywhere can use it (even the unbanked could with our token RoseCoin) and start earning money immediately using the Whispers Host app or find a host to connect with using the Whispers Live app, we encourage you to try both!. We have the highest payouts to hosts and the best bonuses for users. We will also offer an in-app wallet that allows the users to manage their RoseCoin, our native cryptocurrency of appreciation.

4. Is Whispers just another dating app?

No, while we think Whispers is superior to other dating apps, there are many use cases for our platform that does not limit it to only dating! For example, making friends abroad, finding travel companions, getting advice, etc. the opportunities are endless. Each host can create their own personally branded store for whatever services they can offer, at no upfront cost or running expenses. As for dating, we think live video is even better than in person contact (at least initially), it's much easier to dictate terms, safer, less of a time investment and always on demand.

5. How is Whispers better than other dating apps?

Compared to the spam and scams from mobile dating apps or the catfishing and monthly subscriptions on dating sites, our pay as you go system is the best way to date. You get what you see, have access to curated featured profiles, don't have to worry about being rejected, and have a lot more control and anonymity than traditional options. Also, Whispers gives you access to anyone in the world at any time, no more limitations. It's time to stop wasting energy and get straight to the point of making real connections.

6. Why would I pay to use Whispers to meet people?

In modern times, meeting new friends and companions has become more difficult with in-person interaction declining drastically. Younger generations have adapted quickly to technology and moved beyond traditional ways of doing anything. This is especially evident in the social media and dating space where Instagram, Tinder and seeking arrangement sites have all become hugely popular when their very existence would have been unthinkable 20 years ago. While some may have their reservations for paying to talk to someone, it really is no different from buying someone a drink or dinner for giving you their time as a gesture of gratitude. That is why our native RoseCoin is the cryptocurrency of appreciation and our app will provide the best platform to connect hosts and users. Compared to online dating, Whispers is safer, faster and more interactive. Why waste time on spam, fake photos, swiping and text tag when you could have a real time connection on demand?

7. Why host on Whispers?

Unlike live streaming broadcasts on other apps, Whispers hosts get paid for all of their time and do not have to beg for gifts from viewers. Everything is under your control with no minimum broadcasting times so you can maximize your own schedule. Also, because of our proprietary technology and low overhead, we can pay hosts a much higher percentage of their hourly rate. This provides the easiest, fastest and most profitable way for anyone, anywhere in the world to earn a substantial side income just by talking to someone else.

8. Who should host on Whispers?

Anyone can host on Whispers and we encourage all to try. All you need is a good internet connection and a smart phone. Our platform eliminates any startup costs or running expenses for the host so they can immediately focus on building their personal brand and following to generate a consistent income.

9. How much can I make as a host?

In traditional broadcasting channels, revenue is usually ad based and very back loaded, meaning it takes the host a long time to become popular enough to earn marketing dollars. However, social media and live-streaming apps have innovated new ways for content creators to earn money for their talents, namely through product placement and in-app gifts. Unfortunately their payments are sporadic and opaque, with the best being 40% of revenue generated. We have gone one step further and reinvented the old 1-900 calling model with pay as you go pricing for real time connections that allows us to pass on 65% of each call to the host, much higher than our competitors.

10. How do I get paid?

There are many ways to get paid using our app. If you have a paypal account, you can withdraw there. Or for more anonymous and faster transactions, you can choose to be paid in RoseCoins at the exchange rate listed. Then you can convert the RoseCoin to any other currency using our wallet or keep it for future use.

11. Is Whispers anonymous?

Anyone can create a Live or Host account and if you use a credit card or bank to transact with the app we will not save your info. But if you use RoseCoins in the app then your transactions will be faster, cheaper and completely anonymous.

12. What is RoseCoin and why should I use it?

RoseCoin is the first cryptocurrency utility token specifically designed for the livestreaming market. It is used as a token of appreciation within our Whispers app for tipping hosts. There will also be a discount when using these coins instead of fiat which benefits both the user as well as the host. Other advantages include anonymity, security and lower transaction fees. Also, because of the decentralized nature of the coin, anyone in the world will have easy access to use it. The total supply of the token is pre-programmed so you do not have to worry about inflation and after more people adopt the currency, we will continue to add new benefits such as special VIP perks and memberships to real life events. We will also expand the token's ecosystem by partnering with other live streaming platforms to make the coin acceptable across a variety of entertinment services. Before the rise of blockchain technology and smart contracts, this would not have even been possible. Creating an anonymous, non-inflationary, and frictionless global currency will be beneficial for everyone participating in our industry.

13. Why are you doing an ICO?

An ICO is an initial coin offering and has become the newest, easiest and fastest way support innovative projects as well as generate widespread interest for a new product. Anyone can participate, not just the wealthy. Also since RoseCoin is a utility token, it will not be considered a security. Contributors get credits to use in the Whispers Live app, creating a network effect that will be beneficial for everyone involved. Also unlike most other ICOs, this is backing an actual functioning product which will have users immediately!

14. How much does it cost?

Whispers is free to use for both the caller and the host. RoseCoin will be sold for 0.001 Ethereum in the ICO. The rate for each host is set by them but can be negotiated in the app as well. Also, we charge no additional transaction fees for using our wallet.
